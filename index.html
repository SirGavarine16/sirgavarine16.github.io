ABOUT ME

Just another smartass willing to do anything to win

I'm Daniel, a trying-to-be full stack developer born in La Paz, Mexico. Currently studying a bachelor's degree in Software Development Engineering at the Autonomous University of Baja California Sur, and I'm a proud wild cat.

But the fun does not end when it comes to other applications, I have also studied Python, a bit of C#, C++ and Java, I have also tried developing API Rest with NodeJS and Express.

As still a student, I'm trying to get involved in as much software projects as I can. So far, I've been worked with the development of web apps and android apps using React and React Native.

My main goal to achieve is to reach San Francisco and work as a developer for some big company. But this journey is all about learning, and it's amazing what you can do with a computer.
